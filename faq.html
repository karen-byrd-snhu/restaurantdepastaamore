Restaurant Questions

  • Does Pasta Amoré have special events?

    • If we have the event available on our calendar, we would be happy to help you celebrate your special event.

  • Are there Holiday Hours?

    • We are close on Christmas Eve night and all day Christmas. All other holidays are per restaurant choice, so please be sure to call before heading that direction.


  • Does Pasta Amoré cater?

    • We will be happy to cater and event. There will be transport and staffing charges included with the meal price. Please contact the GM or Chef at either location.

  • Does Pasta Amoré provide military and first responder discounts?

    • We provide 50% off to the first responders/military in uniforms. If they come in off duty we will provide 20% to the active member and 10% to family members.

  • Are there celebration discounts offered?

    • Although we don't provide a discount for any celebration, we will be happy to give the guest of honor a custom dessert.

Menu Questions

  • What steps does Pasta Amoré take for food allergies?

    • With the exception of a few dishes (lasagna, cannoli, soups, gelato, etc) that need to be prepared in advanced. We prepare everything homemade. Our pasta is made every morning along with the sauces. If you have an allergy we will be more than happy to make it how you can enjoy the meal.

  • Offer family meals? What is offered?

    • Pasta Amoré does not provide a family style meal. Everything is per person, but the meals are large so it is fine to split the items.

  • Do you have gluten free menu items?

    • There are plenty of items that are gluten free on our menu. We make pasta every morning and include two types of gluten free. Just ask your server and they will let you know which ones are on the menu for that day.
