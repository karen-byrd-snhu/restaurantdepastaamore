Restaurantè de Pasta Ammorè

We at Restaurantè de Pasta Amorè wanted to give an update of what our restaurants and team members are doing to continue to serve you while making an impact in our community.

Taking Care of Our Community

Our restaurants are small business in which our team memebers live in the communities. We are invested in our neighborhoods and will always strive to give back and show our support.

  • Keeping our Restaurants Clean and Safe

    • Our stringent safety and cleanliness standards include following a comprehensive cleaning and sanitizatin process that includes disinfecting all hard surfaces, doors and handles, and we have increased the frequency of cleaning and disinfecting of all high-traffic areas. We reemphasized our internal training to focus on frequent and proper hand-washing and require all tema memebers to wash their hands every 20 minutes and wear clean gloves.

  • Supporting our Team Members

    • We are proactively communicating and sharing up-to-date information with team memebers on the best ways to keep themselves, their families and our guests, safe and healthy. To help prevent the spread of the virus, we're requiring anyone who feels sick or has a fever to stay home. We've also provided resources to answer or team members' most frequently ask questions.
